This version departs from the dark-on-dark approach. A warm cream background (#FAF6EE) signals approachability and editorial warmth — qualities important for a firm that wants to feel expert but never intimidating. Deep navy (#14233F) provides full authority in text, cards, and navbars. The muted gold (#B48A3C) is deliberately restrained: it appears only in eyebrows, decorative lines, and micro-accents. This keeps gold meaningful — when you see it, you pay attention.

Fraunces is an optical-size variable serif with soft, rounded forms and spectacular italics — it brings warmth to headlines while maintaining the editorial authority expected of a financial firm. Inter handles all UI text with exceptional legibility at small sizes and a humanist neutrality that never fights the display type. Together: trust through heritage (serif), clarity through modernity (sans).

09 — Foundation

Design Principles

Decisions made on purpose. Each principle is a constraint that shapes every visual choice in v2.

01

Autoridad cálida

Cream backgrounds and soft radius values make the firm feel approachable, not cold. Authority comes from type hierarchy and restraint — not from intimidating dark surfaces.

02

Gold como señal

The muted gold (#B48A3C) appears only in eyebrows and decorative lines. It marks "pay attention here" — never scattered for aesthetic interest alone. Scarcity makes it meaningful.

03

Jerarquía por contraste

Navy type on cream, cream type on navy: two modes, two contexts, zero ambiguity. Every element knows which surface it lives on. There is no third mode.

04

Humanidad redondeada

Border-radius of 12–18px and pill buttons signal accessibility and warmth. The firm is rigorous with numbers, but never rigid with people. Round forms close the distance.
