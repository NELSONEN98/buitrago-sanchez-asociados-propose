The palette is anchored by warm gold (#C9A84C) against deep navy (#0D1F35) — a classic prestige pairing used in law, finance, and advisory across Latin America. Gold conveys authority and warmth without coldness; navy provides the gravitas expected from a firm handling sensitive financial information. Together they communicate: expert, trustworthy, and premium. The single-accent approach keeps the palette disciplined — this is a brand that doesn't need to shout.

Playfair Display brings editorial elegance to headlines — its high-contrast strokes echo the tradition of printed financial documents and legal instruments, signaling expertise and permanence. Open Sans handles body copy and UI text with exceptional readability at small sizes, with a humanist warmth that keeps the brand approachable despite its formal tone. The serif-sans contrast creates a hierarchy that is both visually rich and functionally clear.

09 — Foundation

Design Principles

Decisions made on purpose. Each principle is a constraint that shapes every visual choice.

01

Confianza primero

Every visual decision reinforces credibility. The restrained palette, generous whitespace, and formal typeface all communicate: this is a firm you can trust with your most sensitive financial information.

02

Claridad sobre decoración

Dense financial content demands tight hierarchy. Labels, eyebrows, and spatial grouping guide users to what they need. Decoration never obscures meaning — every element earns its place.

03

Elegancia contenida

Two colors, two fonts, sharp corners. A focused palette reads as refined expertise rather than visual noise. We don't add more to impress — we remove until only the essential remains.

04

Discreción visual

Dark backgrounds, low-opacity accents, and understated hover states mirror the confidentiality the firm promises its clients. Nothing screams — everything implies. The design reflects the brand promise.
